Hi there, I'm Jesse. I spent the last five years experimenting with making my own barefoot sandals. I feel I have found the right design and materials and would like to share the result with you.

Contents of the kit

  • Sole material
  • Cord
  • Hole punch tool

How to make the sandals

  1. Cut out the outline of your foot
  2. Punch the holes in the sole material
  3. Thread the cord through the holes
  4. Adjust the cord so that it fits your foot
